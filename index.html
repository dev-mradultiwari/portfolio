01 — About

A bit about me

I'm an engineering student at G.L. Bajaj Group of Institutions in Mathura, currently in my third semester of a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering. My first semester ended with an 8.55 CGPA, my second semester's result is still pending, and my third semester is where I currently live — somewhere between assignments, lab files, and trying to actually understand what I'm coding instead of just making it run.
